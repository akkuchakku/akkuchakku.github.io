Burning (2018) - Remake

In our production of our remake of Burning (2018), our primary aim was to accurately replicate the original as effectively as possible, given our limited budget and time constrains. The main focus of this clip was on the long-take dance sequence performed by the character Hae-mi at the climax of the film. Some immediate challenges this presented us, included: The need to film the entire thing at sunset, which we managed by preparing and practicing as much as possible beforehand, having to coordinate both actor and camera movements for the long take, for which we had one group member guiding both people as there was no need to record sound for this section and finally, replicating the jazz piece that accompanied the dance, for which I arranged a piano cover of the original. In achieving these goals, we aimed to preserve the emotional weight and aesthetics of the original scene.
