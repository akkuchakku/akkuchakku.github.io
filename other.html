Flashback

For this task, we were to create a 'flashback' scene. We decided to open with a person trapped in a locked room, then have a flashback implying that they were being followed. Then after the flashback, it is revealed that this paranoia was fabricated by the character, and they had been in the room for mundane reasons, being locked in by accident. As we had no access to sound recording equipment for this excercise (beyond the camera's built in recorder), we elected to focus on shot types and visual storytelling.

Interrogation

For this task we created an interrogation sequence, with the focus here on lighting. Our aim here was to ensure that both characters were well lit in a dark room.
