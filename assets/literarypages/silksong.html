A World You Gave to Bug and Beast

As someone who typically writes fiction, this was my first major creative non fiction piece. Hollow Knight: Silksong released at the beginning of September 2025, after a wait of seven long years and so it occupied most of my thoughts as classes began. One thing that I had always wondered about was why so many people, myself included, were enraptured by a game about a bunch of bugs, so this work is an attempt to deconstruct my thoughts on this question. Some of the dates used within this piece may or may not be accurate, since aside from game release dates, everything else comes from my own memories. I also described some experiences, such as using a Needolin on the apparitions within the mist and watching Daily Silksong News, which were not actually personal experiences but experiences of my friend, but thought it necessary to add them anyway to convey the affect of my experiences. Another tiny inaccuracy is that implied that the Underworks area could be accessed before reaching the Citadel when realistically it could not. I did this since the lore implications of this area more thematically match the other areas that I mention in the same sentence.
