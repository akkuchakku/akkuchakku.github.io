The Affects of Post Horror: Get Out and Sinners

This is a critical essay discussing the nature of the post horror film subgenre with specific reference to Jordan Peele's Get Out (2017) which was the set text for the post horror section of this course. Additionally the text draws some comparisions between Get Out and Ryan Coogler's Sinners (2024) which had I had watched earlier that year.
