How 'Self Narration' Helps Outer Wilds Tell It's Story

For this course we were encouraged to research ideas in narratology which branched from content taught in classes, so I decided to look at one of my favourite narrative games, Outer Wilds (2019), and how it uses a concept of "emergent narrative" to allow players to narrate the story of the game world themselves.
