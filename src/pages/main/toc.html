Tower of Cards

Tower of Cards is a turn based rougelike game which I developed for my HSC Software Design and Development Major project. I had chosen to use the Pygame engine for this work, since it was lightweight, gave me full control over what visuals I could display as the engine essentially just allowed me to render shapes and images on screen. The simplicity of Python3 and the Pygame engine perfectly fit the smaller scale of this independent project. Development for this game took place over the course of six months, during which I was able to create a project proposal, and develop multiple models which evolved into the different features built into the game. The grid based movement was inspired by other games such as Polytopia, while general level progression as well as the different player classes and abilities were influenced by games such as Soul Knight.
