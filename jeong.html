Jeong

Logline: In this poignant and bittersweet exploration of loss, memory, and love, a family sets out on a day trip to honour the memory of their departed loved one, unearthing cherished memories and recalling the healing power of remembrance.
Jeong is the very first film that I contributed to, playing an extensive role in preproduction, directing, set design and sound recording/design. With this film, we wanted to tell an emotional story that would engage audiences and showcase our skills as new filmmakers. Jeong features a wide variety of shot types, and uses specialised equipment such as a smoke machine to tell its story.
This film has gone on to win ‘best picture’ at the ARTS1064 end-of-term screening in Term 2 of 2023 and has been selected for the Sydney Lift-Off Film Festival.
