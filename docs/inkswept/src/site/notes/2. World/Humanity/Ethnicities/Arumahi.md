The Arumahi are an ethnic group originating from the central region of [[2. World/Humanity/Places/Nations/Threscya/Threscya\|Threscya]]. They formed two major civilisations around the [[2. World/Environment/Geographic Features/Rivers/Kymerean River\|Kymerean]] and [[2. World/Environment/Geographic Features/Rivers/Arumotes River\|Arumotes]] Rivers, one of which collapsed and was remade into the city of [[2. World/Humanity/Places/z_Historic Nations/Union/Yvanthryl\|Yvanthryl]], and became the heart of the new [[2. World/Humanity/Places/z_Historic Nations/Union/Union\|Union]]. The [[2. World/Environment/Geographic Features/Rivers/Arumotes River\|Arumotes]] civilisation however remained a fairly minor settlement within the Union, until after [[2. World/Humanity/Important Events/Pralayehena\|Pralayehena]], upon which the local nobility were divided into the two houses of [[1. Characters/House Hykar/House Hykar\|House Hykar]] and [[1. Characters/House Genovar/House Genovar\|House Genovar]], who were given rule of their respective twin city states which the settlement had slowly developed into. The Arumahi make up the largest ethnic proportion within [[2. World/Humanity/Places/Nations/Threscya/Threscya\|Threscya]].
