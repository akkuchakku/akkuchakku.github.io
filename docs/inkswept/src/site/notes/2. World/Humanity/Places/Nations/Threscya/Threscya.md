### History
The Threscyan Empire was founded on the day of [[2. World/Humanity/Important Events/Pralayehena\|Pralayehena]] by the [[1. Characters/Major Antagonists/Zypherien Alokyen\|Divine]]. Threscya specifically refers to the nation of Threscya which is a member state of the empire of the same name, and legally an equivalent peer to other nations such as Prydeshah and Toheren. However in practice, the Threscyan ruling class controls the entire empire and this distinction doesn't actually have any effect. 

### Territory
The kingdom of Threscya comprises of the entire territory of the historic [[2. World/Humanity/Places/z_Historic Nations/Union/Union\|Union]] as well as some early territories acquired within the Divine's first few years of conquest, including land from the region of [[2. World/Humanity/Places/Nations/Hanlyeng/Hanlyeng\|Hanlyeng]] which was added to the [[2. World/Humanity/Places/Nations/Threscya/Northern Province/Northern Province\|Northern Reach]], the surviving east side of the [[2. World/Humanity/Places/z_Historic Nations/Dyzhe/Dyzhe\|Kingdom of Dyzhe]] which became the [[2. World/Humanity/Places/Nations/Threscya/Western Province/Western Province\|Westward Province]]. Some of the land the Divine took from the newly formed country of [[2. World/Humanity/Places/Nations/Toheren/Toheren\|Toheren]] was used to form an [[2. World/Humanity/Places/Nations/Prydeshah/Prydeshah\|ethnostate]] for the Prydeshah, while the rest was absorbed into the [[2. World/Humanity/Places/Nations/Threscya/Eastern Province/Eastern Province\|Eastern Province]]. As of the modern day, all countries under the Drakhon have been absorbed into the empire as a whole; despite nations such as Toheren and [[2. World/Humanity/Places/Nations/Pey Aehtya/Pey Aehtya\|Pey Aehtya]] still technically under independent governance they are subservient to Threscyan power and pay taxes to the Divine. 

The [[2. World/Humanity/Places/Nations/Threscya/Crownlands/Holy Citadel\|Holy Citadel]] is on paper an independent territory from the nation of Threscya, governed directly by [[1. Characters/Major Antagonists/Zypherien Alokyen\|God]] and is the capital of the empire, however since the Threscyan kingdom vs Threscyan empire is non existant in practice, the Citadel are essentially the capital of both Threscya and the empire simultaneously. 

### Ethnicity
The primary ethnicity of Threscya is comprised of the three ethnic groups of three kingdoms which colluded to form the [[2. World/Humanity/Places/z_Historic Nations/Union/Union\|Union]]: the [[2. World/Humanity/Ethnicities/Arumahi\|Arumahi]] people who lived within the [[2. World/Humanity/Places/Nations/Threscya/Central Province/Central Province\|Central Lands]], the [[2. World/Humanity/Ethnicities/Ehyros\|Ehyros]] from the [[2. World/Environment/Geographic Features/Mountain Ranges/Obsidian Mountains\|Obsidian Mountains]] and the [[2. World/Humanity/Ethnicities/Nyven\|Nyven]] of the [[2. World/Humanity/Places/Nations/Threscya/Basin Province/Basin Province\|Basin]]. Over the years migration has shifted this demographic somewhat. The Capital of [[2. World/Humanity/Places/Nations/Threscya/Crownlands/Sel Amaryn\|Sel Amaryn]] has the largest [[2. World/Humanity/Places/Nations/Prydeshah/Prydeshah\|Prydeshah]] population outside the country itself, as there were a few Prydeshah who did not wish to migrate to their new nation in the days after Pralayehena.
